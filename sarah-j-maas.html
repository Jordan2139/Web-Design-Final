THE MAASVERSE

Throne of Glass

Throne of Glass is nothing short of epic, not just in its plot but in its emotional depth. From Celaena’s journey of self-discovery to the masterful foreshadowing and political intrigue, every part of this series builds toward something greater.

Maas delivers shocking twists and a web of interconnected relationships that make this saga unforgettable. From Celaena’s victories to the subtle mysteries, every book peels back a new layer.

Art by @sage.and.other.herbs

A Court of Thorns and Roses

A Court of Thorns and Roses brings beauty and darkness together in one immersive, gripping world. Feyre’s relationships and her journey across the magical courts elevate this series to greatness.

Feyre’s evolution, her bond with Rhysand, and the vibrant world-building make ACOTAR an unforgettable escape. Maas threads mystery and foreshadowing into every moment.

Art by @alicemariapower

Crescent City

Crescent City takes readers into a modern, urban fantasy where magic, ancient powers, and conspiracies collide. Bryce Quinlan’s journey is one of loss, redemption, and discovery.
