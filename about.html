About Me!

Hi I’m Megan! I’ve loved books and stories my whole life. I’m very passionate when it comes to them, and I hope my website can serve as a way to give ideas on what to read next and as a place to find solace among other readers with similar tastes.

My Favorites

After reading constantly for a couple years now, I can say I’m a huge fan of fantasy, especially if it has a touch of romance. I love the magic systems, world-building, politics, and the stakes of life or death. My favorite series is Throne of Glass by Sarah J. Maas. I also adore her A Court of Thorns and Roses series. My favorite “book boyfriend” is Dorian Havilliard—he’s just utter perfection!

Discovering My Love for Reading

I started reading chapter books in kindergarten—my first love was Purple Princess Wins the Prize. Growing up, I adored receiving books as gifts. Some of my favorite childhood series were My Sister the Vampire and Waterfire Saga, which definitely sparked my love for fantasy!

Other Hobbies/Interests

Beyond reading, I enjoy crocheting, knitting, drawing, and painting. I’ve made cardigans, scarves, book sleeves, and more. I also love playing Minecraft, Animal Crossing, and my all-time favorite game, Zelda: Breath of the Wild. My favorite music artists include One Direction, Harry Styles, and Taylor Swift—I’ve been a fan since middle school!
